## What's expected of you

Your AI competencies are defined in the [Product Competency Rubric](https://www.notion.so/thisisartium/1c834860cd298122a8bcf3d32aaa384e?v=1c834860cd29815caf27000c92ae0152) in Notion. AI skills are integrated into existing PM categories:

- **Product Thinking**: AI Solution Design, AI Strategy & Opportunity Identification, AI User Trust, AI Ethics/Safety/Governance
- **Product Delivery**: AI Evaluation & Quality
- **Drive**: AI-Augmented PM Practice

## Staying current

AI moves too fast for static documentation. For current landscape questions, ask an LLM directly:

- *"As a product manager building AI-based software, what has changed in the last two weeks in the AI space that affects me?"*
- *"What are the current best practices for evaluating LLM outputs in production?"*
- *"Compare the latest capabilities of Claude, GPT, and Gemini for [my specific use case]"*

For Artium-specific context, check `#ai`, `#pm-only`, and `#agentic-coding` in Slack, and the Epoch Sessions series.
